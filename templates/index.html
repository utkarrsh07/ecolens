Platform Overview

What EcoLens is designed to do

This prototype is designed to support environmental analysis, planning conversations, report-card interpretation, and public-facing communication by turning raw indicator data into usable insights.

Data Interpretation

Reads structured environmental datasets and converts grade-based indicators into comparable analysis metrics.

Insight Generation

Identifies stronger categories, weaker categories, priority areas, and key findings for rapid review.

Audience Translation

Produces summaries tailored for analysts, policy discussions, and public communication needs.

Scalable Concept

Can later be expanded to support live API feeds, multi-dataset dashboards, maps, and conversational querying.
